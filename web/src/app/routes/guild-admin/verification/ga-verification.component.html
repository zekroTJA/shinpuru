User Verification

Beta

This feature is currently in beta state and therefore subject to bug occurences, future changes and extensions. If you notice any issues with the feature, please file an issue on the issue tracker.

When enabled, users are required to verify their account after joining the guild. They will receive a DM with a link to the verification page where they need to log in and solve a captcha to proof that they are not a bot. Until the verification is done, the user is timeouted on the guild and will be automatically kicked after a time period of 48 hours.
